# Signal Processing Mathematics (EchoPose V3)

The EchoPose pipeline does not merely rely on deep learning to solve WiFi sensing. It utilizes a strict, multi-domain mathematical pipeline to clean the signal *before* the neural network ever touches it.

## 1. Advanced Denoising (The Wavelet Pipeline)
Traditional WiFi pose systems use standard moving averages and median subtraction to locate moving targets. This fails spectacularly when chairs are moved or fans are turned on. 

We utilize **Daubechies Wavelets (db4)** to decompose the raw amplitude signals into distinct high/low frequency subbands, mathematically filtering out the environmental noise profile via Spectral Subtraction while retaining the micro-doppler shifts generated by human limbs.

## 2. Adversarial Robustness
If a user stands behind a wall, the direct line of sight (LOS) path is destroyed. 
Our `RobustCSIProcessor` calculates the Coefficient of Variation (CV) across the subcarriers:
`CV = Var(Amplitude) / Mean(Amplitude)`

If `CV > 0.8`, the system detects severe frequency selective fading and automatically triggers **Multipath Exploitation**, converting the delayed signal bounce vectors into primary feature vectors using an Inverse FFT Power Delay Profile (PDP).

## 3. Disambiguation (The Cocktail Party Problem)
When multiple people walk in a room, their Doppler signatures blend into a singular messy wave. We collapse the CSI tensor into a normalized velocity space and apply **DBSCAN Density Clustering** to mathematically slice the waveform apart based on physical momentum thresholds, allowing the neural net to focus on exactly one person at a time without getting confused.

For codebase specifics, see `inference/pipeline/advanced_denoise.py` and `inference/research/disambiguation.py`.
